"The new website design doesn't help" (This is called the null hypothesis)

Real Website Example - Step by Step

Scenario: Testing a new "Buy Now" button

Old button: 100 sales per day
New button: 130 sales per day (30% increase!)

The P-Value Question:
"Let's PRETEND the new button is no better. If that's true, what are the chances we'd accidentally see 30% more sales?"

P = 0.02 means:
"If buttons were equally good, there's only 2% chance of seeing this 30% improvement by random luck"

Our conclusion:
"2% is too unlikely to be just luck. The new button must ACTUALLY be better!"
