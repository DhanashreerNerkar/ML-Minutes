üìß Email Spam Detection

Scenario: You have 100 actual spam emails in your dataset

Model Results: Your algorithm flags 90 emails as spam

Reality Check: Of the 90 flagged emails, 75 are actually spam (15 are false positives)

75%

Caught 75 out of 100 actual spam emails

Trade-off: Higher recall = catch more spam but risk blocking legitimate emails. Higher precision = fewer false alarms but might miss some spam.

üéØ Key Takeaway

Recall answers: "Of all the actual positives, how many did I find?"

Precision answers: "Of all my positive predictions, how many were correct?"

The Trade-off: Usually can't maximize both - improving one often hurts the other!
