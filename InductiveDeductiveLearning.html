üéì Key Takeaways

üîç Inductive Learning

Direction: Specific ‚Üí General
Process: Observe examples ‚Üí Find patterns
Strength: Discovers unknown patterns
Weakness: Not 100% certain
ML Usage: 95% of machine learning!
Examples: Classification, regression, clustering

üßÆ Deductive Learning

Direction: General ‚Üí Specific
Process: Apply rules ‚Üí Logical conclusion
Strength: Guaranteed correct (if rules are correct)
Weakness: Need to know rules beforehand
ML Usage: Expert systems, rule engines
Examples: If-then systems, logic programming

üí° The Big Difference:

Inductive: "I've seen 100 swans and they're all white, so all swans are probably white"

Deductive: "All birds have feathers, swans are birds, therefore swans have feathers"
